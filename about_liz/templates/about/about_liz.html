About Liz

Liz is a warm and colorful character, with a wealth of creative experience.

Her background is in interior design, humanitarian aid and personal styling, with an emphasis on building confidence and self-esteem. Liz has qualifications in NLP, Style Coaching, TAT, TFT, and IET Healing.

She went to college, at 50 and achieved a degree in Business & Entrepreneurship, followed by post grad diplomas in Innovation, Design Thinking and Enterprise at UCD and Trinity college, to build her expertise in Design Thinking. She recently completed a Masters degree in Digital Entrepreneurship.

Liz is passionate about lifestyle - the day-to-day life that each person leads and her unique blend of qualifications helps individuals to design their futures, despite life challenges and to move forward with optimism and a plan.

Liz brings a wealth of life experience to each consultation and has a wide network of associates to provide any services she may recommend.

Her clients range in age from 19 - 70, female and male. Each client is treated with kindness and great respect and the creativity brought to each challenge is individual rather than systematized.

On a personal level, Liz has been married to her husband Philip for 38 years and is mum to three young adults. She enjoys creative pursuits of all kinds and is a huge believer in personal reinvention and life long learning.
